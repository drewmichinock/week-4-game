Crystals Collector


Rules
  1. The game selects a random number at the start of the game
  2. There are four crystals to the right. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your Player Score
  3. You win the game by matching your total score to the Random Number, you lose the game if your Player Score goes above the Random Number
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it
  5. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal
